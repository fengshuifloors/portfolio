HOME BEACH BOAT BIKE CRABS


Last year,after the pandemic hit. I decided to buy a pontoon boat. I made this site to show some of the things I did over the summer. There are links above to some of the pics I took during the the past year.


I moved here about two years ago, and live about 10 minutes from the bay, and 20 minutes from the beach. I go fishing a good bit. On the bay, I fish for flounder and blue's. On the beach, I fish for striper's and rockfish. That brings us to the crabs. The season starts around the end of June until the middle of October. Unfortunatly that means right now. I usualy catch about two or three bushells every week. I have to give alot to the neighbors, because there's so many.


When I'm not on the boat fishing or crabbing, I'll go out and ride my e-bike. There are around 500trails around here, so there might be alot of pics. I was never real interested in bikes before, but once I rode an e-bike. I had to get one of them. I go about 30 miles on a charge, so it lasts prety much all day. It's half bicycle, half motorcycle, and does about 23mph. When you go on the smaller trails, it can get a little scetchy. You can only do about 5mph, but I've only layed it down about 2 or 3 times. Don't wory I'm OK.
